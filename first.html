INSTRUCTIONS FOR ALGEBRAIC EXPRESSION

  • Use 'n' as a parameter for your algebraic equation
  • You can make use of following symbols:
    1. Arithmetic operators - '*' , '+' , '-'
    2. Power operator - '^'
  • Increment and decrement is not allowed
  • Logical operators are not allowed
  • Log can be used in this form - log(n),nlog(n)
  • Enter the arithmetic expresion in sorted order of powers e.g. 3*n^100+4*n^90

Big oh notation is used to find a curve that is the upper bound of the given expression of time complexity.


Big Omega notation is used to find a curve that is the lower bound of the given expression of time complexity.


Big thetha notation is used to find a curve that bounds the curve on both sides.
